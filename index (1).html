How it works
01

Landmark detection

MediaPipe maps 33 precise body points — joints, hips, shoulders — directly in your browser. Nothing leaves your device.

02

Angle calculation

Joint angles are computed from landmark coordinates using vector mathematics — the same technique used in sports science research.

03

Gait scoring

Each metric is benchmarked against elite runner reference ranges to produce actionable feedback and an overall form score.

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is uploaded anywhere.
